Privacy Policy

Effective Date: October 6, 2025 | Last Updated: October 6, 2025

Your privacy matters to us. ZoneTrain is committed to protecting your personal information and being transparent about how we use it. This policy explains everything you need to know about your data when using our AI-powered running coaching platform.

1. Introduction

ZoneTrain ("we," "our," or "us") provides AI-powered running coaching and personalized training plans through our website, mobile applications, and WhatsApp-based coaching communications. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information.

2. Information We Collect

Personal Information:

  • Name, email address, and contact information you provide during registration
  • Account credentials (password is encrypted and never stored in plain text)
  • Payment and billing information (processed securely through third-party payment processors)
  • WhatsApp phone number (only with your explicit consent for coaching communications)
  • Profile photo and preferences

Fitness and Health Data:

  • Heart Rate Variability (HRV) readings you manually input
  • Training activities and performance data from Strava (with your authorization)
  • Running metrics: pace, distance, heart rate, elevation, splits
  • Training zone analysis and progression metrics
  • Workout responses and wellness questionnaire data
  • Goals, preferences, and injury history

Technical Information:

  • Device information (type, operating system, browser version)
  • IP address and approximate location
  • Usage patterns and interaction data (pages viewed, features used)
  • Cookies and similar tracking technologies

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide Personalized Coaching: Generate AI-powered training recommendations based on your fitness data and goals
  • Analyze Training Data: Process your HRV, Strava activities, and performance metrics to create customized workout plans
  • WhatsApp Coaching: Send daily HRV prompts, training suggestions, and motivational messages (only with your consent)
  • Process Payments: Handle subscription billing and payments securely
  • Improve Our Services: Enhance our AI algorithms and service quality through aggregated, anonymized data analysis
  • Communicate: Send important service updates, support responses, and feature announcements
  • Legal Compliance: Meet legal obligations and protect our rights
  • Security: Detect and prevent fraud, abuse, and security incidents

4. Information Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information. We may share your information only in the following limited circumstances:

  • Service Providers: Trusted third parties who help us operate our services, including:
    • Payment processors (Razorpay, Stripe)
    • Cloud hosting providers (Google Cloud, Firebase)
    • WhatsApp Business API provider
    • Email service providers
  • Connected Services: Platforms you choose to connect (like Strava), only to the extent you authorize
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law, court order, or to protect our rights, safety, or property
  • Business Transfers: In connection with a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets (with notice to you)
  • With Your Consent: Any other sharing that you explicitly authorize

5. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information:

  • Encryption of data in transit (TLS/SSL) and at rest
  • Secure authentication using bcrypt password hashing and JWT tokens
  • Regular security audits and vulnerability assessments
  • Access controls limiting employee access to personal data
  • Secure cloud infrastructure with automatic backups

Important: While we use robust security measures, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security.

6. WhatsApp Communications

Our WhatsApp-based coaching service operates with your explicit opt-in consent. We use the WhatsApp Business API to send:

  • Daily HRV prompt requests
  • Personalized training recommendations
  • Workout reminders and motivation
  • Important account notifications

You can opt out at any time by replying "STOP" to any WhatsApp message or updating your preferences in your account settings.

7. Data Retention

We retain your information for as long as necessary to:

  • Provide our services and maintain your account
  • Comply with legal obligations (tax, accounting, legal requirements)
  • Resolve disputes and enforce agreements

Account Deletion: You may request deletion of your account and associated data at any time. We will delete your data within 30 days, except where we're legally required to retain certain information.

8. Your Rights and Choices

Depending on your location, you have the following rights:

  • Access: Request a copy of your personal data
  • Correction: Update or correct inaccurate information
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your account and data
  • Data Portability: Receive your data in a machine-readable format
  • Withdraw Consent: Opt out of WhatsApp communications or data processing
  • Object: Object to certain data processing activities
  • Restrict Processing: Limit how we use your data
  • File Complaints: Contact data protection authorities

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at zonetrain@zohomail.in

9. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

We use cookies and similar technologies to:

  • Remember your login session and preferences
  • Analyze usage patterns and improve our services
  • Provide personalized content and recommendations

You can control cookies through your browser settings. Note that disabling cookies may limit certain features.

10. Third-Party Services

Our service integrates with third-party platforms:

  • Strava: To access your running activities (requires your authorization)
  • Google: For authentication and cloud services
  • Payment Processors: For secure billing

These services have their own privacy policies. We're not responsible for their practices.

11. International Data Transfers

Your information may be transferred to and processed in countries other than your own, including India and the United States. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place to protect your information in accordance with applicable data protection laws (GDPR, CCPA, etc.).

12. Children's Privacy

Our services are not intended for users under 16 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe we've collected data from a child, please contact us immediately.

13. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. We will:

  • Post the updated policy on our website with a new "Last Updated" date
  • Notify you via email for material changes
  • Provide a summary of key changes

Continued use of our services after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

Address: ZoneTrain, AP Block, Pitampura, New Delhi-110034, India

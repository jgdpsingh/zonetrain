Shipping & Delivery Policy

Last updated: November 21, 2025

ZoneTrain is a purely digital SaaS (Software as a Service) platform. We do not sell or ship physical goods.

Delivery of Services

Upon successful payment for a subscription:

  • Instant Access: Your account will be automatically upgraded to the premium tier immediately after payment confirmation.

Issues with Access

If you have paid but cannot access premium features, please contact our support team immediately at zonetrain@zohomail.in with your transaction ID.
